Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

PulseTempo uses Apple HealthKit to read your heart rate data from your Apple Watch. This data is used solely to synchronize the metronome's tempo with your heart rate in real-time.

2. Data Usage

We do not store, upload, or share your health data. All processing happens locally on your device. We do not use your health data for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Third Parties

Your health data is never sold or shared with third-party brokers, advertisers, or any other entities.

4. Your Consent

By using the heart rate sync feature, you provide explicit consent for the app to access your heart rate via the standard iOS/watchOS permission prompt. You can revoke this access at any time in the "Health" app settings on your iPhone.

Medical Disclaimer: PulseTempo is intended for musical and fitness purposes only. It is not a medical device and should not be used for medical diagnosis, treatment, or monitoring.
